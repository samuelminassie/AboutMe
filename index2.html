About Me

Sam Minassie is a results-driven Salesforce professional with advanced certifications including Salesforce Advanced Administrator, Agentforce AI Specialist, Marketing Cloud Account Engagement (Pardot) Specialist, App Builder, and Administrator. He specializes in optimizing CRM ecosystems to drive revenue growth, improve marketing performance, and enhance operational efficiency. With deep expertise in declarative development (Flows, validation rules, automation), user management, data governance, and system integrations, he designs scalable, business-aligned solutions that empower teams and strengthen data integrity.

In his current role as Senior Salesforce Administrator and Data Lead at the Pembina Institute, he modernized an outdated CRM architecture, implemented Account Engagement (Pardot) Lightning, and introduced AI-powered record summarization to improve productivity and insight generation. He leads cross-functional requirements gathering, oversees complex data migrations and integrity initiatives (including duplicate prevention and weekly merge processes), and develops organization-wide training programs that have significantly increased user adoption and CRM proficiency. Previously at CTM Biomedical, he contributed to over 30% year-over-year revenue growth through analytics-driven insights, opportunity automation, and data optimization initiatives.

With a foundation in business management and communications, he brings a strong blend of technical expertise and stakeholder engagement. He thrives in Agile environments, translates complex requirements into streamlined systems, and is passionate about leveraging automation, reporting, and cross-functional collaboration to support mission-driven organizations and scalable growth.
